About anndaan

We are a platform which aims to eliminate hunger

We are students of Manav Rachna International Institute of Research and Studies , Faridabad. We have developed this platform as a hackathon project. Our aim is to make sure that no person in India sleeps on empty stomach.

The problem we address

Everyday, more than 20 crores kilograms of food is wasted in India. On the other hand, over 20 crore people sleep empty stomach in India every single day. This is very ironic and sad at the same time. Almost a kilogram of food is wasted per hungry stomach every day. Also , the NGOs willing to donate food often lack the resources or connections needed to do so.
